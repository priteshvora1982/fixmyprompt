## Detailed Description (up to 16,000 chars)

**Stop wasting time on bad AI responses. FixMyPrompt makes every prompt count.**

Most people get mediocre results from ChatGPT and Claude — not because the AI is bad, but because their prompts are vague. A 5-minute task turns into a 2-hour loop of re-prompting, tweaking, and frustration.

FixMyPrompt fixes that at the source.

---

### How it works

1. **You type your prompt** in ChatGPT or Claude as usual.
2. **FixMyPrompt detects** if the prompt is missing key details.
3. **A smart balloon appears** showing your prompt's quality score and what's missing.
4. **Answer 3 quick questions** — tap chips, no typing needed.
5. **Your prompt is rewritten** — more specific, better structured, ready to send.
6. **Get a dramatically better AI response** in your first try.

No prompt engineering knowledge required. No learning curve. It just works.

- 🎓 **Academic** — research papers, essays, literature reviews

- 🙋 **Personal** — wellness, goals, habits, self-improvement

### Why FixMyPrompt

**Problem 1 — The Time Sink:** A task that should take 5 minutes turns into a 2-hour rabbit hole because the AI didn't understand what you really needed. FixMyPrompt gets you to the right answer on the first try.

**Problem 2 — The Hidden Risk:** A vague prompt gets a generic, sometimes subtly wrong answer. You might not even notice — and act on it. FixMyPrompt adds guardrails, structure, and expert framing to every prompt before it's sent.
