The Problem

Most people get poor AI results.
Not because AI is bad — but because prompts are vague.

Two costly problems, one root cause: most people don't know how to write a prompt that gets results.

⏳

The Time Sink

A 5-minute task turns into a 2-hour loop of re-prompting, tweaking, and clarifying. You go in circles because the AI didn't understand your initial vague request.

⚠️

The Hidden Quality Risk

A vague prompt gets a generic — sometimes subtly wrong — answer. You might not even notice and act on it: a business plan with weak assumptions, a marketing email with the wrong tone.

🧠

Prompt Engineering is Hard

Writing expert-level prompts is a skill that takes months to learn. Most people shouldn't need to become prompt engineers just to get useful answers from AI.

How it works

From vague to powerful in 60 seconds

FixMyPrompt works silently in the background. It only speaks up when your prompt needs help.

1

You type your prompt

Write whatever comes naturally. Don't overthink it — that's what FixMyPrompt is for.

2

We detect what's missing

After 2.5 seconds of inactivity, we analyse your prompt and score it. If it needs work, a balloon appears.

3

Tap chips to answer. No typing needed. 30 seconds total. Questions are specific to YOUR prompt, not generic.

4

Your prompt is rewritten

We send your improved prompt back to the input box — structured, specific, and ready to deliver a great response.

5

Send & get great results

Hit send. Get the high-quality AI response you needed from the very first message.
