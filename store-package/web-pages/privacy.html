Privacy Policy

We built FixMyPrompt with a privacy-first philosophy. Here's exactly what we do and don't do with your data.

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: we don't store anything about you.

✅ No prompts stored
✅ No account required
✅ No personal data collected
✅ No tracking
✅ No ads, ever

1. Who We Are

FixMyPrompt is a Chrome browser extension that helps users improve their AI prompts on ChatGPT and Claude. This Privacy Policy describes how FixMyPrompt ("we", "us", or "the extension") handles information when you use the extension.

2. What Data We Collect

✅ Short answer: We collect no personally identifiable information. We do not know who you are, where you are, or what you typed.

Prompt text

When you use FixMyPrompt, your prompt text is sent to our backend API (hosted on Railway) in real-time to generate improved versions. This data is processed and immediately discarded. It is never written to a database, log file, or any persistent storage.

Domain classification

Your prompt is classified into one of 8 categories (e.g. "technical", "creative_writing") to generate relevant questions. This classification happens on your device and is never sent to us independently.

Session state

The extension stores minimal state in your browser's local storage — specifically, whether the improvement balloon has already been shown in the current conversation. This data never leaves your device.

3. What Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any account information
  • We do not store your prompts or AI conversation history
  • We do not track which websites you visit (outside of detecting chatgpt.com and claude.ai)
  • We do not use cookies for tracking
  • We do not collect IP addresses in a personally identifiable way
  • We do not sell data to third parties — ever
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not use analytics services that track individual users

4. How Your Prompt is Processed

When you click "Improve" or answer questions in the FixMyPrompt balloon, your prompt text is sent to our backend server via an encrypted HTTPS request. Here's what happens:

  1. Your prompt is received by our API server.
  2. It is passed to the OpenAI API (GPT-4) to generate improved text or clarifying questions.
  3. The improved prompt is returned to your browser.
  4. Your original prompt is immediately discarded from our server — it is not stored anywhere.

Our server processes requests statelessly. There are no user accounts, no user IDs, and no way to associate a request with a specific person.

5. Third-Party Services

OpenAI API

We use the OpenAI API to generate improved prompts and clarifying questions. Your prompt text is shared with OpenAI as part of this process. OpenAI's use of data submitted via their API is governed by OpenAI's API data usage policies. As of 2024, OpenAI does not use API inputs/outputs to train models by default.

Railway (Hosting)

Our backend API is hosted on Railway. Railway may retain standard server access logs (IP addresses, timestamps, request sizes) for operational purposes. These logs are used for debugging and security only and are not associated with your identity or prompt content.

6. Children's Privacy

FixMyPrompt is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the extension, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions about this, please contact us.

9. Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our support page.
